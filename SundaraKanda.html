In Sundara Kanda, you will find a description of the arrival of Lord Rama and his allies in Lanka; the superhuman feats of Hanuman including setting Lanka ablaze are also mentioned. Sundara Kanda is the only chapter of the Ramayana in which the hero is not Rama, but rather Hanuman. The work depicts the adventures of Hanuman and his selflessness, strength, and devotion to Rama are emphasized in the text
