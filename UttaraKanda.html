In Uttara Kanda you will find the details of the exile of Sita, the birth of Luva and Kusha, their battle with Rama to catch hold the horse for Ashvamedha Yajna, Sita's final refuge in the arms of Mother Earth, and Rama's ascent to Heaven. Uttara Kanda is the last Book of the great epic Ramayana and it deals with the return of Rama to Ayodhya and his coronation to the throne. The reign of Rama in Ayodhya has also been narrated in this Book along with the second ordeal of Sita.
