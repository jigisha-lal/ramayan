Ayodhya Kanda describes the exile of Lord Rama into the forest for 14 years for the sake of his father, King dashratha's honour. Ayodhya Kand is one of the important kands in Ramayan and narrates the arrangements for Lord Rama's coronation and his exile into the wilderness for a period of 14 years in order to fulfil his father's wish. ...
